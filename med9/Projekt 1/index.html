Leopard

Food

  1. Various Ungulates
  2. Various Primates
  3. The biggest animal they eat is the eland

Place

Leopards are mostly resident in south Africa and in parts of southwestern Asia and many parts of India. They went extinct in many places in southeast Asia, Arabian Peninsula and parts of Africa

Lifespan

The lifespan of a leopard is between 12 and 17 Years.
